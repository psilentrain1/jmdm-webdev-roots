About Us.

At Roots Community Bakery, we believe great bread tells a story. Our story begins with local farmers, handcrafted techniques, and a passion for bringing people together through the simple joy of baking.

Our Promise.

  • 100% locally sourced ingredients
  • Freshly baked artisan breads daily
  • Inclusive menu with gluten-free and vegan options
  • Connecting community through the art of baking

Our Offerings.

Artesian Breads

From crusty sourdough to hearty whole grain, each loaf is carefully crafted to bring out the richest flavors and textures. Our breads are made from grains sourced within 50 miles of our bakery, supporting local agriculture and ensuring maximum freshness.

Pastry Selection

Indulge in our rotating menu of sweet and savory pastries. Each week brings new surprises, from classic croissants to innovative gluten-free tarts that don't compromise on taste.

Community Workshops

Learn, bake, and connect! Our monthly baking workshops are perfect for home cooks, baking enthusiasts, and anyone curious about the magic of bread making. All skill levels welcome.
